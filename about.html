A little bit about me, my skills, and hobbies.

Hello!

My name is Ana Avila. I am a UX Designer with a background in Computer Science. I’ve had the opportunity to work in Front-End Web Development and UX Design. From both fields, I feel most passionate about problem solving, responsive design, organization, maintenance, scalability, and creating for people. I believe flowcharts and information architecture are key to deliver great visual designs for our users, and to adapt our designs in different devices and formats for consistent experiences in any screen size.

- Ana
